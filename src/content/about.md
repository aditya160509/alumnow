## The question behind AlumNow

Choosing a university or course is too important to be reduced to a ranking table. AlumNow gives JBCN students a direct line to alumni who can share the parts of their experience that a prospectus cannot.

## What a good session feels like

It is focused, honest, and specific. You bring the questions. Your mentor brings lived context. Together, you leave with a clearer next step — whether that means comparing two courses, preparing an application, or understanding the life around a university.

## Built around trust

Alumni profiles are connected to the JBCN community and reviewed before they appear in the network. Students and families get a considered, transparent way to choose who they want to learn from.
